Identity / Manifesto

We build sound-led visual systems for artists, collectives, and cultural brands. From sonic direction and DJ programming to visual identity and campaign art, we stage ideas like living posters.

Rooted in Swiss grid precision and anti-design instinct, our work embraces contrast: disciplined composition, ecstatic color, and tactile imperfection. We design for memory, movement, and meaning.
