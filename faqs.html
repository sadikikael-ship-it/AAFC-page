Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. Omar works by appointment to keep the environment calm and reduce wait times.

How long does grooming take?

Most appointments are 1.5 to 3 hours depending on coat condition, size, and service level.

How do I get the most accurate quote?

Text a recent photo and your dog’s breed/weight along with desired service.

Can I request specific add-ons?

Absolutely. Choose from nails, ears, sanitary trim, paw trim, teeth, or combo add-on.
